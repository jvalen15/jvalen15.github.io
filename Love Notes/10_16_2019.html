Dear, Angel

I know you are sleeping right now (like a little angel), but I just had to give you something sweet to wake up to. I am covertly writing this love letter while I am in my Animal Ethics class! Hehe. I actually started this love letter yesterday, but then I got tired and passed out at like 8 P.M. (I am such a copycat)! I like that I can write these love letters as a little way of showing you how much I love and care about you. Sometimes I enter deep-thought thinking about in what ways you might be successful in life - The possibilities are endless - and I like imagining you as an NPR reporter, a newspaper journalist, or a podcast/radio show host. I just think you are going to be so successful in life, my love. I'm going to be right by your side through it all!

"I look at you and see the rest of my life in front of my eyes."

We just have so much in store for us, Julia. I cannot wait to travel the entire world with you. Maybe one day we won't even be living in America. What if we settled down in Ireland or something? I think that would just be so cool. Well, anyway honey, I just love you so stinking much, and I hope you enjoy the heckle out of this love letter. I'll be yours forever.

Love, Your Boy (I'm yoursssss)!
